The Problem
Everyone has a physical habit they want to correct — hunching their back, looking down when they walk, crossing their arms, shuffling their feet. You know you do it. But knowing isn't enough. You forget the moment you step out the door.
The Solution
You describe your habit in your own words. Habit Nudge runs silently in the background and fires a notification at your chosen interval — every 30 minutes, every hour, or every 2 hours. The notification shows your exact words back at you. That 2-second moment of awareness is enough to trigger self-correction.
When you feel you've genuinely broken the habit, you mark it as done. It moves to your Past Habits as a completed win.
Features

Your words — describe the habit exactly how you feel it, no templates
Silent background nudges — runs without opening the app
Quick actions — tap "Fixed it" or "Too late" directly from the notification
Start time control — set when nudges should begin each day
Frequency control — every 30 min, 1 hour, or 2 hours
Past habits — a record of every habit you've corrected
Progress tracking — see your wins and misses over the week
No account needed — all data stays on your device
AI simplification — describe your habit naturally, AI condenses it into a punchy notification phrase (coming soon)
