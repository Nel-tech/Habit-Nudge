How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No dashboards to fill in. No streaks to obsess over. Just small, quiet corrections.

01
✍️

Describe your habit in your own words

Type it naturally — "stop checking my phone in the morning" or "drink water before coffee". No templates.

02
🔔

We nudge you silently throughout the day

Quiet notifications at smart times remind you of your intention — not an alarm, just a gentle check-in.

03
✅

Catch yourself. Correct it. Mark it done.

One tap. That's all. No journaling, no reflection prompts. Just acknowledge and move on.

Features

Built to stay out of your way

Every decision made to reduce friction, not add to it.

🤫

Silent background nudges

Notifications that whisper, not shout. Timed throughout the day so they actually land.

🗣️

Your words, not ours

Write habits the way you actually think about them. No forced categories or templates.

⚡

Quick notification actions

Mark done directly from the notification. You never have to open the app.

📊

Progress tracking

Simple streaks and completion history. Enough to feel good, not enough to stress you out.
